Get to Know the CryoPen®

A new age in cryosurgery is here. The CryoPen® is a one of a kind tool for physicians everywhere interested in providing a broader range of service to their patients while enjoying the simple, safe, and effective benefits only the CryoPen® brings to this field.

The CryoPen® Cryosurgical System stays in the desireable temperature range throughout the duration of the procedure. Compared to other methods of cryosurgery, the CryoPen®'s temperature range stays most consistent during this period of use.

Select appropriate re-usable tip (sizes 3, 5, 7, and 10mm). Once tip is applied to the CryoPen’s® hand-held module, the physician is ready to begin treatment. It's a simple as that.

Indications for Use

  • Intended Use: Cryosurgical unit used for ablative type surgical technique.
  • Indications For Use: Multiple organ systems, wide range of disease, viral, pre-malignant and malignant tissue.

The CryoPen® system is designed to ablate tissue by the application of extreme cold temperatures. The CryoPen® system is ideal for Primary Care, Internal Medicine, Dermatology, Plastic Surgery, OB/GYN, and Podiatry.
